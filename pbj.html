Peanut Butter and Jelly Sammich

Description

The best peanut butter and jelly sammich you've ever had.

Ingredients

  • fuggin bread. Two slices.
  • fuggin peanut butter. Creamy.
  • fuggin Jelly. Grape or strawberry.
  • Butta Knife

Steps

  1. Spread a copious amount of peanut butter on both slices of bread.
  2. Spread your jelly of choice on one slice of bread on top of your previously spread peanut butter.
  3. Apply your slice of bread with peanut butter only to the top of the jelly side of the other slice of bread.
  4. Enjoy.
